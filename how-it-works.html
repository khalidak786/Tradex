How Tradex Works

1Browse Live Prices

Access real-time commodity prices directly from our main dashboard:

  • View comprehensive product listings with live prices
  • Filter products by commodity type and specific categories
  • See immediate availability and stock levels
  • Monitor price changes in real-time

2Product Details

Click on any product row to access detailed information:

  • Complete product specifications
  • Detailed pricing information
  • Available quantities
  • Delivery terms and conditions
  • Quality certifications and documentation

3Trade Using Quote ID

Each product listing includes a unique Quote ID:

  • Quote ID is your reference for the specific trade
  • Use this ID when contacting our team
  • Ensures accurate and efficient trade processing
  • Helps track your order throughout the process
Important: Always reference the Quote ID in all communications to ensure quick and accurate service.

4Complete Your Trade

Follow these steps to finalize your trade:

  • Contact our team via WhatsApp or email with the Quote ID
  • Confirm quantity and pricing
  • Review and agree to terms and conditions
  • Complete documentation and payment process
  • Track your order through delivery

5After-Trade Support

Our service doesn't end with the trade:

  • Track shipment status in real-time
  • Access all trade documentation online
  • Get support for any questions or concerns
  • Maintain a history of all your transactions
